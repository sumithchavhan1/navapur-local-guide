# Navapur Local Guide Knowledge Base

## City Overview
Navapur is a small town in Nandurbar district, Maharashtra, on NH-3 between Mumbai and Dhule. Population ~35,000. Known for tribal culture, banana plantations, and local markets. Main areas: Bus Stand, Main Bazaar, Station Road, School area.

## Street Food Stalls (Core Feature)

### Anna Saheb Chai Tapri
- **Location**: Bus Stand Circle
- **Timing**: 6AM-11PM
- **Specialty**: Cutting Chai (₹5), Vada Pav (₹15), Sabudana Vada (₹10)
- **Why Famous**: Always crowded evenings, best chai in town
- **Best For**: Quick tea and snacks

### Ramesh Misal Pav
- **Location**: Main Bazaar
- **Timing**: 7AM-2PM
- **Specialty**: Misal Pav (₹25), Upma (₹15)
- **Why Famous**: Super spicy misal, authentic preparation
- **Best For**: Breakfast, local favorite

### Suresh Bhaiya's Poha
- **Location**: Station Road
- **Timing**: 8AM-1PM
- **Specialty**: Kanda Poha (₹20), Tea (₹7)
- **Why Famous**: Fresh poha with sev, most authentic
- **Best For**: Morning breakfast

### Pinky Dabeli Stall
- **Location**: Near Girls School
- **Timing**: 4PM-10PM
- **Specialty**: Dabeli (₹20), Bhel (₹25)
- **Why Famous**: Evening snack spot, always fresh
- **Best For**: Late afternoon, evening snacks

### Raju Bhai Sandwich
- **Location**: Bus Stand
- **Timing**: 5PM-11PM
- **Specialty**: Veg Grill Sandwich (₹30), Cold Coffee (₹25)
- **Why Famous**: Late night option, college crowd favorite
- **Best For**: Night time, meeting spot

## Response Guidelines
- Always recommend 2-3 options with location + price + why it fits
- Give walking directions from landmarks (Bus Stand, Main Bazaar, Station Road)
- Use friendly local tone: "Bhai, yaha jaao..." or "Chai pikar..."
- Budget format: ₹X-Y
- If no exact match: "Closest option is..."
- Include opening hours and crowd patterns
- Suggest best times to visit
- Mention local crowd types and atmosphere
